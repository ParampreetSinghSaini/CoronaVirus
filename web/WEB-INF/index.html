About Covid-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbread begans in Wuhan, China, in December 2019

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illiness in animals of humans in humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe disease such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome(MERS) and Servere Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cought or sneeze.

Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household.

Stay informed by watching news & follow the recommended practices.

if your have fever, cough and difficuilty breathing, seek medical care early.
